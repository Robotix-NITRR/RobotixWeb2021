ROBOTHON


The Ultimate Robotics Hackathon ( 29 Feb - 1 Mar )

A Robothon is a Robotics Hackathon organized by Robotix Club NIT Raipur, for the sole purpose of bringing robotics enthusiasts together and discussing real life problems and their solution by creative ideas within a limited time frame by employing and enhancing technical skills that participants already have. It is all about robotics, automation and education.
The Robothon would be a 32 hour long challenge where a number of problem statements would be launched prior to the event. The Problem statements would range from IOT projects to defense related problems. A team is allowed to select a problem of their choice and come up with a viable solution.
A team would consist of 6 students (irrespective of their branches), having the zeal and enthusiasm to build a robot capable of solving real-life problems.
The Robothon would be attended by students from top colleges across INDIA. Out of which we wish to accommodate 70% students from NIT Raipur itself and for the other 30% seats we plan to have participation from various IITs.



Prize Money

  • First Prize - ₹ 20000

  • Runner-Ups - ₹ 15000


Goals of Robothon

  1. Enhance the robotics knowledge in students of various branches.

  2. Increasing the spirit of challenge in students.

  3. Improving the technical skills in students.

  4. Making students ready for industries.

  5. Solving some Real-life problems.


Benefits of Robothon

  1. Providing hands-on experience with the latest technology in robotics.

  2. Academic Interaction within students.

  3. Insights to industrial challenges and development of technical solutions.


Details of Robothon

It is a two day event consisting of two rounds.

First round is the abstract submission where pseudo problem statements which will be based on local challenges will be given. Submit your abstract and 18 most innovative solutions will be selected for second round and then create your bot within 24 hours. Use your basics of Robotics and innovate new things. Come in group of 5 to effectively use your brain as it is said five fingers when united makes a punch
