ROBOFEST'2020

29 February - 1 March

Robofest provides an opportunity for the students of the institute to practically apply their knowledge in Robotics by use of their problem solving ability. By giving the students an opportunity to interact with the field specialists we hope to boost each students’ entrepreneurial spirit. The Fest incorporates all this under one roof and provides the participants with a firsthand experience with Robotics.
Robofest will be organized in collaboration with team TED-X NIT Raipur, to encourage the spirit of collaboration between students. Collaboration is extremely helpful as it saves ample of time and resources of organizing event as everything is a joint effort.
Mentorship and Guidance will also be provided to the participants by industry experts to accelerate their potential. This will give each student a great opportunity to learn about the various future scopes in Robotics and prepare them for it.
